Runner type peanuts are the most popular peanuts in America. They rose to prominence in the early 1970s with the introduction of a new variety called the Florunner. The Florunner quickly took off and it and other runner peanuts have since grown to make up the majority of cultivated peanuts, beating out the other major variety, bunching peanuts.
